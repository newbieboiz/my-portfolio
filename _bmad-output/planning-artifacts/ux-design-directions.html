Design Notes

Authenticity

The most committed coding-vibe direction. Developers will love it. The terminal IS the design — no pretense, no decoration.

Maximum developer signal

Concern

Recruiters may not "get it." Maya scanning QR on her phone sees a terminal — does she recognize quality, or confusion?

Audience mismatch risk

Hybrid Potential

Works beautifully as an Easter egg or entrance animation — but probably not as the only navigation paradigm.

Best as accent, not base
